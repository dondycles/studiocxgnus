ABOUT

CXGNUS is an Anime NFT Project headed by YUTA, with Co-Founder Suoh, and developed by Dondycles and Shibakinu, in the production of Studio CXGNUS. The studio is led by Head Artist Suoh with a wonderful team composed of Freshcakes and Nemurin. The lore’s OST/music is produced by Xius

Ever wondered what it feels like to be part of an anime story? Minting one means transporting a character between NFT space and the world of CXGNUS.

Join us as we embark on an adventure and a new age of storytelling with five thousand ESPers in the space!

MANIFESTO

Make Memories, Build Together.

The space is full of surprises and innovation every single day, bringing opportunities to learn and discover new things. We want to create a place- specifically, a home where everyone can learn and grow together, enjoying what life has to offer, while being true to our natural and authentic self without the fear of being seen by others. This is the difference that we want to make.

Here at CXGNUS, we are a collection of fictional characters reminding you to appreciate little things and enjoy the adventures of life.
